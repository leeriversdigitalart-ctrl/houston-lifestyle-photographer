The Approach

Documentary. Candid. Honest.

I'm Lee Rivers — a Houston-based documentary lifestyle photographer and PPA member. My approach is simple: I show up, I observe, and I capture what's actually happening. No posing, no forced smiles, no awkward "look over here" moments.

Whether I'm on the sidelines of a run club race in 90-degree Houston heat or watching a couple in their natural element, my job is to disappear into the background and let the real story unfold.
